About About Trivia Time

Trivia Time is a fun game that challenges you to test your knowledge with various questions. You can play as many times, view your scores and track your GPS location

Features

  • Play 5-question trivia quizzes
  • A mixure of easy, medium and hard questions
  • Quiz timer for each question
  • Scores saved automatically
  • View quiz history with date & time
  • Location saved using GPS

Built using Ionic Standalone Framework
